## Central Conflict

**External conflict:**

> The tangible, visible struggle. What is at stake in the world of the story? What must the protagonist do or prevent?

**Internal conflict:**

> The psychological or moral struggle. What must the protagonist confront within themselves? What must they learn, accept, or overcome?

**How the conflicts intersect:**

> The best historical fiction binds the personal to the political. How does the protagonist's internal journey mirror or contrast with the external historical events?
